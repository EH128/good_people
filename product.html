More than just a calendar
MyPlanner allows you to automatically generate custom calendar events. All you need to do is enter your tasks and subtasks for a given time period, and let MyPlanner handle the rest!

Based on the tasks and subtasks you provide, MyPlanner will connect to your Google Calendar and automatically create calendar events based on the deadlines and expected time commitments you’ve provided.
